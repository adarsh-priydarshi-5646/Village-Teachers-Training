About Us

At Village Teachers Training, we are dedicated to providing advanced technology training to teachers in rural areas. Our mission is to empower educators with the skills they need to transform the learning experience for students in their communities.

Our Programs

  • Basic Computer Skills: Learn essential computer operations and tools.
  • Online Teaching Tools: Master modern platforms like Zoom and Google Classroom.
  • Interactive Learning Techniques: Engage students with dynamic content and methods.
  • Advanced Internet Usage: Explore online resources and integrate them into your curriculum.
